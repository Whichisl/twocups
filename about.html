Meet the Owner

Two Cups & Resto was founded by Gourav Teli, a passionate coffee enthusiast and culinary visionary. With a deep love for bringing people together over great food and conversation, Gourav set out to create a space where community and creativity could thrive. His journey into the café world stems from years of exploring different flavors, cultures, and the joy of sharing meals.
